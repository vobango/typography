This is just text

And yet it is so much more. Through typography text can tell a story by itself or enhance the emotion and purpose of text. In the last 25 years or so the personal computer has become a common household item containing thousands of fonts and other text-editing tools. Combined with the power of the Internet, everyone can share their thoughts, stories and teachings with billions of people. But it seems that with the excitment of sharing ideas the art of typography is often forgotten. However reading is one of the most time consuming activity in our daily lives (whether online or on paper) and we should aim to make our written words as beautiful, professional and most importantly, as readable as possible.
